    Attributes - Product landing pages

    With the modern URLs in v10 we have introduced the ability to add parameters to the URL.

     

    Imagine a page with a variant that uses 'attributes' to sell the variant in different colors and sizes.

     

    Traditionally the URL might have looked like this: 

     

    www.yourdomain.com/p-3-bike.aspx

     

    The URL would land the shopper on a page where he or she could select size:

     

     

    With today's modern URL structure, your URL will look like : www.yourdomain.com/product/3/bike ...

     

    ... but now you can add your conditions as parameters.

     

    So, if you send a shopper an email with an attractive hyperlink, you can link them to 

     

    www.yourdomain.com/product/3/bike?size=large&color=red

     

    or

     

    www.yourdomain.com/product/3/bike?color=blue

     

    or

     

    www.yourdomain.com/product/3/bike?variantid=4

     

    ​If you are 'feeding' products to Google and Bing, you will know that this is a fabulous advance - you can feed the red product, and the blue product (that might have different images, and/or different PRICES) and the shopper will land on exactly the right combination of attributes.
