    EBizCharge Integration

     

    Integrating with EBizCharge

     You will have received the required integration elements from EBizCharge when signing up for their payment gateway services. This article assumes that you have already setup the integration.

     

    *options for EBizCharge will not exist out of box.*

    ​

    Obtaining your credentials

    Please contact the EBizCharge support team to obtain your credentials via email at: support@ebizcharge.com

     

    ​

    Configuring the store

    1. Login to the AspDotNetStorefront admin console.
    2. You will now be directed to the Dashboard. Click Configuration, located at the top right of this screen.

       

       

       

    3. A dropdown menu will be displayed. Under the Payment section, click Payment Methods.

       

       

       

       

    4. You will now be directed to the Site Setup Wizard. Your browser should automatically take you to a section titled Credit Cards & Other Payment Methods, located roughly two-thirds of the way down the page.

       

    5. Below the Credit Cards & Other Payment Methods section, you should see another section titled Payment Processing Solutions. Under this section, set the Payment Gateway to EBizCharge if it is not already selected. Click the configure link to the right of EBizCharge.

       

       

       

    6. A popup window titled Configure EBizCharge, shown below, will appear on your screen. Enter the values you obtained from EBizCharge Merchant Services for the Gateway ID, Gateway Pin, and Gateway URL.

       

       

       

    7. Click Save and Close.
    8. Finally, click the radio button next to EBizCharge and then click the Save button.
