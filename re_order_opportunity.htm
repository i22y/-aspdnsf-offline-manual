    Re-order opportunity

    AspDotNetStorefront v10 introduces an anchor tag https://yourdomain.com/account#OrderHistory which you can use very effectively for links to 'order history' (or 'order tracking') within your store, and also in emails, to invite your customers to return and repeat an order.

     

    When you put an item on sale, or get new stock in, why not email all of your opted-in shoppers to let them know and link them to their earlier orders. Use the Customer Service notes on the order to drop a special message for your customers.

     

    ​

     

    One click will drop a copy of an earlier order straight into the All-in-One Checkout, making it as likely as possible that the order will be completed.

     

    ​Note - kit items will not be re-ordered.
